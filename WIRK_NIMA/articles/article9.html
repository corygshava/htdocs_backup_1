Ants Food Prevention and Control

  • Ants get attracted to food sources, therefore ensure your house is clean, especially the kitchen. Ensure that attracting food substances are covered tightly.
  • Sweep the floor after meals because even the tiniest food substances can attract ants.
  • Always ensure food that is not in the fridge is covered.
  • Avoid leaving dirty dishes in the sink.
  • Always empty the trash regularly.
  • Ensure to keep the trash cans far from your house.
  • Always keep the house dry, because ants will always be looking for a source of water and stay near it.
